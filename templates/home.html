About this page

Benjamin Franklin Said : "If You Fail to Plan, You Are Planning to Fail". What he meant was that basically success doesn't happen by accident.It takes planning, knowing where you are heading and how you will get there. If you fail to do this you may stumble upon a win here or there, but the ultimate success won't be in that stumble.
This page gives you the order in which you should complete your tasks to minimise delays (Objective1), or penalties (Objective2), either you use Dynamic Programming, Metaheuristic, or Hangdson & Moore algorithm(this one is only for the first objective).

The file that you should use must contains 3 lignes

  • First Line : Number of tasks
  • Second Line: The processing time for each task, separated by tabs
  • Last One : The deadline for each task, separated by tabs
